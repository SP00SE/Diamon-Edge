HIT & HR SCORES

Each player is assigned a score from 0–100 predicting how likely they are to get a hit or homer in today's game.

80–100Elite — very high probability
70–79Strong — above-average chance
60–69Moderate — worth watching
0–59Low — limited positive indicators
Confidence Levels "How safe is this pick TODAY?" — a risk filter on top of Hit Score
★ Very ConfidentClean profile — high score, confirmed lineup, soft pitcher, regular starter
✦ ConfidentGood situation with only minor risk factors present
◆ Neutral / Risk PresentMixed signals — decent pick but at least one meaningful risk
▲ Low ConfidenceMultiple risk factors — lineup uncertainty, tough pitcher, or low usage
⚠ High RiskSerious red flags — conflicting signals, avoid or use with extreme caution

STREAK LABELS

Compares a player's last 7 games batting average (L7 AVG) to their full season AVG. The difference is measured in batting average points.

🔥 Hot StreakL7 AVG is 30+ points higher than season AVG — player is on fire recently
❄️ Cold StreakL7 AVG is 30+ points lower than season AVG — player is in a recent slump
— SteadyL7 AVG is within 29 points of season AVG — performing close to their normal level

Example: Season AVG .280, L7 AVG .220 → difference = −60 pts → ❄️ Cold Streak

Hot Streaks Tab — Flame Intensity shown on streak cards

The number of 🔥 flames on a streak card shows how hot the streak is.
